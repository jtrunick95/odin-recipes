The Perfect Chicken Breast

Description

This recipe will show you the CORRECT way to cook chicken breast. No more boring dry chicken breasts for you!

This recipe is for a southwestern, blackened chicken breast. Feel free to use your favorite seasonings in place. The real magic here is how it is cooked.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast of any size
  • High smoke point oil like vegetable, avocado, or pure olive oil
  • Kosher Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Cumin
  • Paprika
  • Thyme
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Chili Powder

Instructions

  1. Pat dry chicken breast with paper towel. Get it as dry as possible. This will ensure we get a nice crust.
  2. Place chicken breast between saran wrap and smash with a mallet or something heavy like a cast iron pan to even out the chicken breast. It should now be an even thickness. This will allow the chicken to cook evenly, resulting in a more moist and juicy breast.
  3. Heat cast iron pan to medium high
  4. While pan heats, season chicken with all seasonings
  5. Add oil to pan
  6. Once oil just barely begins to smoke, add chicken to pan and press firmly on chicken
  7. Allow chicken to cook for about 3-4 minutes depending on thickness
  8. Flip chicken and cook until internal temperature reaches 145-155
  9. Remove chicken and wrap in foil and let rest for 5 minutes. This is where carry over cooking comes in. The chicken will continue cooking and the temperature will continue to rise.
  10. Enjoy!
